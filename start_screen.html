"Rambler Studios"

A space where young and talented individuals explore their creativity, personal exploration and develop their own set of skills. Rambler Studios, is a platform where individuals can desighn their own sustainable path in life.

Rambler founded in 2007 by Carmen van der Vecht, inspired by the unique outfits worn by the people living on the streets in cities like London, Sao Paulo, and Amsterdam. Their unique outfits, worn not just as clothes, but as a powerful expression of creativity and identity. Feeling a deep sense of inspiration, Rambler was founded, to showcase these incredible street styles to the world.

Fast forward to Rambler Studios Amsterdam, the first creative Rambler Studio, nestled in the heart of Zeedijk. Rambler Studio is not just an ordinary studio, it's a place where youngsters (Ramblers) dive into the world of self-expression. Here, they learn to express their creativity, build essential skills, and shape their lives through the canvas of street fashion.

Guiding this creative journey is a team of dedicated Rambler coaches. Their mission? To assist these young Ramblers in designing their path through life. The studio becomes a place of growth, empowerment, and a community where individuals can find inspiration, collaborate with like-minded peers, and bring their visions to life.
